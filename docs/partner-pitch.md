# Partnership pitch — for Surfshark / ProtonVPN / NordVPN

Cold outreach template to get a Gecko-buyer-only extended trial deal.
Two versions: short (subject + first paragraph for LinkedIn / Twitter
DMs) and full (for partner@ / affiliates@ / business@ inboxes).

Customise the `[brackets]` per provider before sending.

---

## Subject line (A/B test these)

- "Distribution partnership — Gecko (home server, ~1k buyers/year)"
- "Bundling [Provider] with Gecko, a self-hosted Netflix alternative"
- "Pre-installed VPN slot in our home-server appliance — open?"

(My pick: option 1. It's specific, it's quantified, it doesn't try
too hard.)

## Tips for sending

1. **Personalise the "Why I'm reaching out to [Provider]" paragraph
   for real.** Generic emails get ignored. Mention something true
   about their offering: ProtonVPN's free tier, Surfshark's
   unlimited-devices policy, NordVPN's specialty servers, etc.

2. **Send to the right address:**
   - Surfshark: `partnerships@surfshark.com` or via affiliate
     dashboard
   - ProtonVPN: `partnerships@proton.me`, or apply at
     <https://protonvpn.com/affiliate-program> first then follow up
   - NordVPN: their affiliate program portal first
     (`nordvpn.com/affiliate-program`), then contact AM
   - Mullvad: skip, no affiliates

3. **Have a backup if first-choice declines:** apply to all 3
   simultaneously. Whoever responds first with a real deal wins.

4. **Be honest about scale.** I deliberately said "300-1500 buyers
   year 1" because anyone with experience reads "10k buyers" as a
   lie. Modest, defensible numbers earn the meeting.

5. **Affiliate program standard rates** (so you know what's normal
   for the ask):
   - Surfshark: $14 per signup
   - NordVPN: 30-40% commission, ~$30 typical
   - ProtonVPN: 35% recurring (better long-term than one-off)
   - ExpressVPN: $13-36

## What to do if they say yes

- Sign up to affiliate program → get unique affiliate URL or
  coupon-code template
- Update `docs/email-templates/welcome.md` with their coupon code
  format and signup URL
- Update wizard's VPN step: prefill provider name, link to signup
  with affiliate-tagged URL
- Mention them on `gecko-web/` landing (logo + "VPN partner" line)
- Use real activation numbers from month 1 to justify negotiating
  better commission at month 6

## What to do if they all say no

- Stick with **Mullvad** as default — no affiliate, no trial, but
  best privacy reputation, easy WireGuard setup. Users know it.
- Or: bootstrap with **PIA** or **TorGuard** which have looser
  affiliate programs and may onboard without a chat.
